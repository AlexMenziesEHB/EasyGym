How it works

Created for you!

The majority of people who come to exercise in a gym do not use a machine in the correct way. Other (new) athletes do not dare to ask questions to employees about the use of the machine and/or the exercise itself. This is to help you!

Watch yourself do it

EASYGYM lets you watch yourself do the exercises in a fun and easy way through the use of the camera with instructions. It's as easy as starting a workout and following the instructions. If that doesn't help watch the bottom instructions under each page!

Easy instructions

Everything is easy to understand! choose one of the workouts provided by EASYGYM and follow the given instructions by using the simulator or watch a given video or even follow the given written instructions. Are you ready?!

Website and look

Do you prefer to look at exercises before or after you head to the gym? With the newly created webiste of EASYGYM? it is as easy as choosing what workout you would like to learn and watch te video. Simpel and easy for you!
