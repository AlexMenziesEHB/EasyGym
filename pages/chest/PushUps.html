Push Ups

Chest Exercise

Watch the video
or start your workout with the simulator This wil show you how to do it

How to do?

Unable to watch the video or use the simulator?
Than take a look in steps how to do the exercise

Step 1

Get down on all fours, placing your hands slightly wider than your shoulders.

Step 2

Straighten your arms and legs.

Step 3

Lower your body until your chest nearly touches the floor.

Step 4

Pause, then push yourself back up.

Step 5

Repeat.
