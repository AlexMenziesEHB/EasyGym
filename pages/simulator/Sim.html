Get Ready

Let EASYGYM allow access to your camera to let the simulator work properly
then follow the steps below so you know how to do it!

Step 1

Before starting the workout make sure to watch the explanation video so you fully understand what to do

Step 2

Place your device on the floor or on a desk where you know the camera can catch your full body

Step 3

Place yourself in front of the camera making sure your full body is visible on screen.

Step 4

Get down on all fours, placing your hands slightly wider than your shoulders and straighten your arms and legs.

Step 5

Lower your body until your chest nearly touches the floor. Follow the bars on the right of the screen to see how far you have to go to count a push up, then push yourself back up.

Step 6

Keep your back straight during the time of the exercise and follow the onscreen feedback or instructions when needed.
